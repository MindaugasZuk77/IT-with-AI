Domains

Make It Memorable & Brandable

Choose a name that is easy to remember, spell, and pronounce. Avoid numbers and hyphens that can cause confusion.

Choose the Right TLD

While .com is most popular, also consider other TLDs like .io for tech, or .AI projects, or country-specific ones like .uk or .de.

Check Trademark Availability

Ensure your domain doesn't infringe on existing trademarks to avoid legal issues. Search the USPTO database for existing trademarks.

Mobile-Friendly & Social Media Ready

Check if the name is available on major social platforms. Shorter names work better for mobile displays and social media handles.

Consider Long-Term Use

Choose a name that will grow with your business. Avoid trends or names that might become outdated.

Protect Your Brand

Consider registering common misspellings and different TLDs to protect your brand and prevent cybersquatting.
